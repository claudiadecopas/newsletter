What a wonderful week of reflection, prayer, and service!

We started off the week with our Lenten Reconciliations, and our 1st through 8th grade students participated in their Lenten Retreats.Thank you to the teachers for planning, organizing, and presenting our children with this experience.

Service was also a significant part of our week with the National Junior Honor Society (NJHS) service project. Together with the collaboration of the entire John Smith School Community, 220 Easter Baskets were assembled with all the donations to benefit His House Orphanage. Thank you to Mrs. Piñeda for organizing this special project, and to all of the teachers and staff for helping in donations and also giving of their time. 

We would also like to thank The Valda Family, along with our guests, for their wonderful presentation on Social Media Safety & Awareness on Wednesday night. 

We are blessed!
